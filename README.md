# Clay Factoria

Create clay automatons using the power of Gaia's life essence!

Clay Factoria adds clay creatures to the world of Orbis. Powered by Gaia's life essence, these little automata can be programmed to perform tasks for you:

- The Clay Trork, although much smaller than a normal trork, is strong enough to haul items around and carry them 
between chests or work benches.
- The Clay Kweebec is proportionally smaller, and isn't strong enough to carry heavy blocks and items around. Instead,
it can be programmed to interact with work benches like the furnace to activate them.

The player's newfound ability to mould life from clay allows them to achieve a level of automation by asking their new friends to work the furnaces, refill and harvest from tanning racks, or just carry items from place to place!

Clay Factoria is built with a core design constraint of adhering to the theme and lore of Hytale. We don't want to just make Factorio or Satisfactory in Hytale, we want to make something truly unique and inspirational to others, and particularly the Hytale team themselves.

We intend to continue developing this mod in the long term. But between jobs, university, and life in general, it's not possible for us to pour our full attention into a mod which is truly a passion project to us.
